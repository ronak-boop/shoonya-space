01 — Platform
// 01
Shoonya OS

The operational intelligence layer. Connects drones, ground sensors, radars, and cameras into a single real-time tactical picture. AI-powered threat detection, asset tracking, and mission orchestration — all running on sovereign infrastructure.

// 02
SWARM-S

Autonomous swarm deployment system. A single operator commands a formation of drones that autonomously navigate, attach to target structures, and execute coordinated payload deployment on command. Designed to remove soldiers from close-range risk.

// 03
MeshLink

Encrypted battlefield communication network operating on 433MHz radio — no WiFi, no cellular, no internet dependency. AES-256 encrypted. Self-healing mesh topology. Designed to operate in GPS-denied and communication-jammed environments.

// 04
ShieldNet

AI-driven perimeter intelligence. Computer vision, thermal imaging, seismic sensors, and radar fused into a single threat awareness layer. Continuous monitoring, automatic classification, and escalation to command. Built for bases, borders, and convoys.
